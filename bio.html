Artist Bio

Kaicong Tang | Bio


Chinese digital artist, and the first generation in the family pursuing Art practice. Kaicong is currently graduating from San Jose State University with a BFA degree in Digital Media Art. He is interested in making digital illustrations that explore human emotion and feeling in a particular environment. His work of art will be using himself as a reference in illustrating the narrative digitally. In the future, his works will continue to expand and collaborate with 3D sculptures and the environment.
